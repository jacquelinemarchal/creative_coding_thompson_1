Welcome!
Here is how to play:

  • Use the keyboard to generate pitches
  • Open your mouth to hear different timbres
  • Move your head to detune pitches

    • Credit: Thank you to Jeff Thompson for his open source Creative Programming 1 and 2 courses on Github and Youtube. I followed along with his video on integrating p5js with Mediapipe Facemesh to start this project. I also adapted the keyboard code from Tim Vallencourt's Assignment for Computational Sound class at Barnard.
